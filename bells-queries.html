How long will it take to receive my wedding film?

You will get your entire wedding film 3 to 4 months following your big day. Each of our films are distinct resulting in distinct production durations. Every film undergoes multiple revisions until we arrive at a stage where we confidently affirm it as our very best work.

I am getting married next year, should I wait a bit longer to enquire? 

We commit to only a few weddings in a year. Therefore we are booked 8 to 9 months beforehand.

How do you go about creating a wedding film?

Our goal is to deliver a wedding film with a refreshing and innovative style while also encapsulating heartfelt, true-to-life emotions. We craft our films emphasizing on content and visuals and not on trends.

Could you provide details about your payment terms?

We require that you pay 50% of the total amount during booking. The rest of the amount needs to be paid on day 1 of coverage. 

What if we want to hire extra videographers?

No. We focus on maintaining clean compositions by keeping obstructions away and individuals carrying cameras are avoided. 

We are not having a grand wedding. Is there still a chance to have you on board?

We do not label ourself as a luxury wedding film company, we are a bunch of passionate film makers who yearn for great stories. Let us know your story and we will create a custom package for you. 

Are you willing to travel to cover weddings?

Certainly. We are ready to travel anywhere in the world to document your wedding.

What is the size of your team?

Depending on the personal needs, the team size is determined. About 3 photographers are sufficient to fully capture a big day.

What can we expect from your team?

You can expect a brief cinematic trailer and a long cinematic highlight film. You can also expect performance/interview videos as well.
